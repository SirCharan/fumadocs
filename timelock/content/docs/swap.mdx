## Introduction
Timelock Swap is the unified liquidity layer of the Timelock Protocol Ecosystem. UniswapV4 with hooks optimizes how liquidity is deployed and reused across the ecosystem powering perps, options and prediction markets.

Timelock Swap enables permissionless markets for any ERC20 token on any EVM chain, enabking spot, perps and options for all ERC20 tokens, enabling leverage for 5000+ altcoins, RWAs, onchain equities etc.

## Liquidity Vaults

Liquidity Providers do 1 click USDC deposit into vaults managed by Timelock AI agents, Charm.fi and market makers (eg Re7, STS Digital). 
Passive yield for LPs without worrying about rebalancing, range selection, active monitoring.

Timelock offers two vault types to match different risk profiles:
### Uniswap Vaults:
- For projects to seed liquidity for enabling perps, options and prediction markets.
- For LPs bullish on the upside of any token and seeking to earn extra risk neutral yield on it.
- For tokens launched through Timelock Launchpad.
- For Uniswap LPs seeking extra yield on their Uniswap positions with the same risk profile.
### Stable Vaults (tUSD):
- For family offices, whales, large funds or retail seeking high yield on their stablecoins.
