## Introduction
Timelock Perps enables perps without liquidations for all ERC20 tokens, on all EVM chains deployed on Timelock Swap.

Democratizes access to leverage for 5000+ altcoins and building rails for leveraging RWAs, onchain equities, gold etc, allowing anyone to create a perps market for their token by simply supplying liquidity to Timelock Swap.

## Core Features

### Liquidation Free Leverage
Retail can now perps without the fear of getting liquidated and without blowing up their entire account

### Oracle Free Design
Pricing natively derived from Uniswap. No oracles involved. No manipulation. No market makers involved.
</Step>
<Step>
### Permissionless Markets
Can enable perps for any ERC token, any EVM chain. Memecoins, RWAs, onchain equity. Everything.
</Step>
<Step>
### Timelock Builder Codes
Enables any project/individual to enable perps on their token and deploy a perps frontend on their own dapp/website and Timelock shares upto 50% of trading fees with them.
</Step>
